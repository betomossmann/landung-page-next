MARIANA BARCELLOS

Welcome to my practice! My name is Mariana Barcellos and I am a licensed and experienced psychologist with a passion for helping people lead happier, healthier lives.

I received my degree in psychology from University of Vale dos Sinos, and have been practicing for 10 years. During that time, I have had the opportunity to work with clients from all walks of life, with a wide variety of needs and concerns.

My approach to therapy is grounded in compassion, empathy, and a deep respect for the unique experiences of each individual. I believe that everyone has the capacity to heal and grow, and my job as a therapist is to provide a safe and supportive environment that allows that process to unfold.

Whether you're struggling with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or just feeling stuck in life, I am here to help. I use a range of evidence-based techniques and interventions to help you overcome your challenges and achieve your goals.

In addition to my work as a therapist, I also offer mentoring and coaching programs for individuals and groups who want to learn more about psychology and personal growth.

I'm excited to work with you and help you create the life you want. If you have any questions or would like to schedule an appointment, please don't hesitate to contact me.
